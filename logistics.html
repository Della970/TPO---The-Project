Our logistics services


We will move whatever you need, to wherever you need, any time, no questions asked.


All liability for any illegal cargo, shall be on the client, which will be stipulated via written contract to be signed before any service is given.


Prices will be relative to the distance, weight, and volume. Insurance will also be charged to the client, since we do not take any interest nor will intend to know the value and contents of the cargo, apart from the required information in order to achieve a succesful delivery.


As with any other service offered by us: Payments shall only be made via crypto currency on selected and secure exchanges.


Failure to pay or to maintain the requirements after the service has been initated could result in seizure of goods for compensation and also permanent termination of any service given.


Disclaimer: Breaking the code of trust created to secure our clients and ourselves is a serious offence and will be dealt with accordingly.
