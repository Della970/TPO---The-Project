Our travel service


We will take you anywhere you want, with whoever you want, any time, no questions asked.


You will travel incognito, on secured aircraft that will arrive to secure locations on the vicinity of the travel destination, from there we will move you again, by land, water or helicopter the exact location stipulated by you.


All liability for any illegal cargo, shall be on the client, which will be stipulated via written contract to be signed before any service is given.


We will not provide the service if we find any evidence of illegal dealings with other persons (such as: Kidnapping). Proof of this will terminate the contract and any future prospect of work by us.


Prices will be relative to the distance, people, weight and volume carried. Insurance will also be charged to the client.


As with any other service offered by us: Payments shall only be made via crypto currency on selected and secure exchanges.


Failure to pay or to maintain the requirements after the service has been initated could result in seizure of goods for compensation and also permanent termination of any service given.


Disclaimer: Breaking the code of trust created to secure our clients and ourselves is a serious offence and will be dealt with accordingly.
